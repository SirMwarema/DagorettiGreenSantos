Our Team

Dagoretti Green Santos has the aspiration of discovering, nurturing, developing and enhancing growth of potential talent in football through youth programs We are have developed a very simple strategy to remove as many youth from drugs, crime and prison. Through our progress feeding programmes and also weekly sports.

Head Coaches...

We have a number of coaches who carry significant responsibility, to players who look to them for so much more than just athletic instruction.They leading training sessions, providing motivation and advice during game play, developing game plans, attending tryouts, as well as scheduling team meetings most of tne time from their own pockets.
