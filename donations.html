Our 12/12 Inter Santos Event

Every year, we host the 12/12 Inter Santos event to celebrate and give back to the Dagoretti community. We donate nonperishable food, hygiene products for girls, school books, and gifts from well-wishers while enjoying soccer games.

Our Commuinity

Our school programs support the SDGs and provide assistance to cover expenses such as school fees, uniforms, books, and food. We rely on help from well-wishers to make this possible. While Kenyan government schools are technically free, there are still compulsory termly and monthly payments for things like the lunch feeding program and tuition fees.

Girls health & Sanitary pads

Santos Foundation prioritizes girls' health and provides education and vital sanitary products to help build community cohesion. Our program is compulsory for girls in the community, as we understand that they often suffer in silence. We are committed to uplifting the girl-child in our community.

Educating Orphans

Our club provides guidance and leadership to orphans from poor backgrounds, helping them see beyond their current challenges. We strive to achieve the SDGs for a better Dagoretti by 2030, with a focus on providing inclusive and equitable education for all age groups from under 9 to under 20. We believe that this is achievable and are committed to making it a reality.

School Outreach

Our Scholarships outreach promotes the development of socio-emotional skills in children, strengthening their school environment through play and sports. We encourage all the kids to go to school those from disadvantage homes are supported from our programs.

Families are encouraged to participate in these activities in order to strengthen their commitment to their kids being in school. This is all done through inter-school linking activities.
