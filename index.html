Foundation Strategy

The foundation strategy of Dagoretti Green Santos is both simple and tactical: to provide the local community with the best possible opportunities for development. Through skills training, teamwork, and community initiatives such as providing school fees for those who cannot afford it, food for the hungry, and cleaning programs to uplift the society, we strive to make a positive impact in our community.

Community Growth

The growth of Dagoretti Green Santos over the years has enabled us to develop aid programs for the community. We strongly believe that by helping one neighbor, the cycle of generosity and community growth can multiply. Our focus is not just on personal growth for the upcoming generation, but also for their families, as we work towards uplifting the community as a whole.

Generational Wealth

Building generational wealth is a fundamental component of personal growth and development, and it starts with aiming for financial stability. At Dagoretti Green Santos, we have taken a proactive approach towards giving back to our community, by setting aside a fraction of proceeds from our online shop sales of team jerseys, soccer balls, team bags, and branded water bottles into a donation bank, to support those who are less fortunate.

Team Consistency

Dagoretti Green Santos, we believe that creativity, hard work, and smart play are essential abilities to turn dreams into reality in football. Through consistent effort, discipline, and perseverance, we have shown that with enough blood, sweat, and tears, one can move from an amateur to a professional league.

Our Mission2010-2030

Our mission at Dagoretti Green Santos is to involve the community in sports, education, health, and poverty reduction initiatives in their sub-location. Through our academy, we aim to develop players who will eventually play for the senior team, and our ultimate goal is to see Santos players dominating national and international clubs.

Our Vision2010-2030

Dagoretti Green Santos, our goal is to create a conducive environment for eradicating poverty through education, sports, agriculture, and other activities. By doing so, we aim to improve the living standards of the people in Dagoretti Sub-location, Nairobi.

School Program2010-2050

We prioritize schooling programs and sensitize parents on the importance of education as a basic right for every child globally. Through our empowerment programs, we provide school fees and supplies for children who would otherwise not be able to attend school, believing that education is the key to unlocking potential and opportunities for the youth in our community.

Community Empowerment2010-2050

Our foundation aims to remove barriers to education and support children in our community to achieve their career aspirations. We recognize that poverty and other factors can create barriers to education, so we work to create a conducive environment for learning and provide resources like books, classrooms, and teaching materials.

Children

It's important to recognize the crucial role emotional support plays in helping children recover from trauma and abuse. By creating a safe space for kids to participate in sports and other activities, our organization aims to break the cycle of poverty and empower disadvantaged youth to succeed.

Poverty

Our aim is to ensure that families facing financial hardship have access to education and vital resources. By doing so, we hope to empower children from underprivileged backgrounds to achieve their full potential, become socially aware individuals, and make positive contributions to their communities' sustainable development.

Community

At our organization, we strive to reduce the number of kids who drop out of school and help young footballers achieve success in life. To accomplish this, we are working to establish an exchange program with other football academies worldwide. By doing so, we hope to provide our players with valuable exposure, enhance their communication skills, and cultivate social awareness.

Man-hood

Our organization is committed to empowering young men to lead responsible, fulfilling lives by providing them with access to education and vital resources. We believe that by reducing the dropout rate of boys from school and equipping them with the tools they need to succeed, we can create a generation of economically and socially empowered men who will make a positive impact on their families, communities, and the world.
