About Us

Dagoretti Green Santos is an Initiative for transformation, was registered in Kenya in 2017 as a community based organization. Santos main objective is to empower the local community through football, Education & training, resource mobilization using Self Help concept, Health, sanitation and environment protection for sustainable development and transformation.

We Make History

Dagoretti Green Santos, we are dedicated to discovering, nurturing, and developing potential football talent through our youth programs. Our goal is to cultivate well-rounded young professionals who will keep the game vibrant and exciting for years to come, while also striving to win trophies and achieve success on the field.

Trophies We Won

Coca-cola Cup Champions 2008

"The Coca-Cola Championship was a challenging competition, with many strong teams vying for the top spot. Despite the tough competition, our team managed to secure victory in the regional cup, earning us the opportunity to represent our county in the next stage of the tournament."

Kiko Cup 2010 Champion

"This league is one of the most exciting and competitive in the region, with several stages leading up to the final cup. Despite the challenges posed by the pandemic and elections, our team was able to maintain an unbeaten record throughout the entire season and ultimately win the cup."

Dagoretti Cup 2011

"Our league is part of the prestigious Dagoretti leagues, and the pride that comes with being a part of this community is truly amazing. We were promoted to this league in 2020, and we were able to maintain an undefeated record throughout the entire season, ultimately winning the league."

Extreme Cup Champion 2012

"Dagoretti Green Santos maintained an unbeaten record throughout the entire season, despite the challenges posed by the pandemic and elections. Our team's hard work and dedication paid off, as we were able to emerge victorious and win the championship. This was an incredible accomplishment for our team and a testament to the skill and determination of our players and coaching staff."

This Is Our Story

"In order to achieve our goal of producing top football talent for professional clubs within and outside of Kenya, Dagoretti Green Santos is dedicated to developing a comprehensive academy that caters to players between the ages of 7 and 25. Our academy is located in Dagoretti Corner and makes use of the facilities provided by the Nairobi City Council, which are well-suited to our needs. We offer scholarships to talented footballers from underprivileged backgrounds in the Dagoretti slums, and we welcome additional sponsorship to help establish a solid financial foundation for our academy."

Building a Foundation

The academy's commitment to developing players from a young age is rooted in the belief that the earlier a player starts training, the greater their chances of success. To this end, the academy provides training programs for players as young as seven, introducing them to the fundamental skills and techniques required to become a successful footballer.

Nurturing Talent

In addition to its focus on early development, the academy also provides comprehensive training for players at all levels of the game. This includes technical training, tactical training, physical training, and mental training, ensuring that players are fully equipped to excel both on and off the field.

Highly Qualified Coaching Team

To support the development of its players, the academy employs highly qualified and experienced coaches, who have a proven track record of success in the sport. The coaches work closely with players, providing personalized training and support to help them overcome their weaknesses and build on their strengths.
